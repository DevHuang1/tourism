Chase the Charm of China

From imperial places to sacred mountains,China invites you to walk through centuries of living history.

Experience the Culture of China

Rich Traditions

From festivals to philosophy,the richness of Chinese culture reflects unity,values and prosperity with the dragon as a symbol of power and good fortune.

Lovingly People

The citizens of China carry the heart of the nation,with grace, resilience and a welcoming spirit. Their warmth and hospitality make every journey a memorable experience, inviting you to connect with the soul of this ancient land.

Chinese Cuisine

From the rich flavors of Peking duck to the delicate dumplings of Shanghai, traditional Chinese dishes capture the nation's centuries of culinary traditions.

Prominent Destinations

Explore China's most splendid locations

Great Wall of China

Hike ancient watchtowers,ride a cable car,or zoom down on a toboggan-the Great Wall offers epic views and a true step into China's past.Each stone tells a story of strength,legacy and breathtaking endurance.

Huairou or Yangqing district,Beijing 4-5 hours

Shanghai

In Shanghai,tradition and modernity collide.Wander the bund for skyline,head up the Shanghai Tower,and explore ancient beauty at Yuyuan Garden.Take a river cruise by night,then shop and snack your way through Nanjiang Road.

Pudong and Huangpu Districts 1-2 Days

Forbidden City

Once home to emperors, the Forbidden City is full of grand halls,secret gardens, and golden rooftops. Walk through majesic gates,explore the opulent palaces,and discover the treasures of China's imperial past which are embedded in the heart of Beijing.

Dongcheng District 3-4 hours

Chongqing

Known as the "Mountain City," Chongqing is famous for its spicy hotpot,stunning night views,and the Yangtze River cruise. Explore the ancient streets of Ciqikou,visit the Three Gorges Museum,and take a scenic boat ride through the dramatic gorges.

Chongqing Municipality 2-3 Days

Lijiang

Lijiang is a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its well-preserved ancient town,traditional Naxi culture,and stunning natural scenery. Explore the cobblestone streets,visit the Black Dragon Pool,and hike in the nearby Jade Dragon Snow Mountain.

Lijiang City,Yunnan Province 2-3 Days
