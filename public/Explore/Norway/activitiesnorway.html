Featured Destinations

Explore Norway's most captivating locations

Northern Lights in Tromsø

Tromsø is ideal for witnessing the magical Northern Lights.Visitors can take guided aurora-chasing tours by bus,snowmobile,or dog-sled,offering unforgettable views of the dancing green lights in the polar sky.

Tromsø 2-3 Days

Hjørundfjorden

It is nestled among the Sunnmø Alps,is perfect for peaceful fjord cruises,kayaking,and scenic hikes.Surrounded by dramatic mountains and tranquil water,it's a hidden gem for nature lovers and photographers.

Møre og Romsdal 1-2 Days

Lofoten Islands

Wonderful Lofoten Islands offer breathtaking scenery with rugged peaks,fishing villages, and white sand beaches.Visitors can enjoy activities like hiking,cycling,kayaking and even surfing under the midnight sun or northern lights,depending on the season.

Nordland 3-5 days

Loen Skylift

It whisks you from the fjord to the mountaintop in just minutes,revealing sweeping views of Nordfjord below.At the top,you can hike,dine or simply relax while taking in some of Norway's most awe-insping landscapes.

Vestland,Western Norway 2-4 hours

Trolltunga

Trolltunga is one of Norway's most iconic hikes,leading to a dramatic rock ledge that juts out 700 meters above Ringedalsvatnet Lake.Adventurers can embark on the full-day hike for a rewarding,panoramic photo opportunity at the edge of the cliff.

Vestland(near Odda) 1-2 Days
