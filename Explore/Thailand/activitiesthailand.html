Premier Destinations

Explore Thailand's iconic attractions

Wat Arun

The Temple of Dawn,is a peaceful place to explore riverside views and detailed mosaic artwork.Take in the scenery and enjoy the calm atmosphere-perfect for a short cultural visit or photo session at sunset.

Bangkok Yai 1-1.5 hours

King Power Mahanakhon

This sky-high tower offers a thrilling experience with its glass-floored SkyWalk.You can relax at the rooftop bar,take photos,or enjoy a meal while admiring Bangkok's Skyline.Go during golden hour for the best view.

Silom,Bangkok 1-2 Hours

White Temple

It is a blend of spirituality and modern artistry.Walk the symbolic bridge,admire the mirrored structures and explore the peaceful temple grounds.More than a temple,it's a living campus of purity,rebirth and Thai contemporary art.

Chiang Rai 1-2 Days

The Grand Palace

It is a masterpiece of Thai architecture,where golden spires,royal halls and sacred temples reflect centuries of history,Explore the revered Emerald Buddha,stroll through elegant courtyards and immerse yourself in Thailand's rich royal legacy.IT'S best to arrive early to avoid crowds and the midday heat.

Phra Nakhon,Bangkok 2-3 hours

Krabi

Krabi is a tropical escape known for its turquoise water,towering limestone cliffs,and pristine beaches.Spend your days hopping,relaxing on beaches,kayaking through mangroves,or rock climbing.It's the perfect blend of adventure and serenity.

Krabi Province,Southern Thailand 2-3 Days
